System: Transactions Service

Description:
The Transactions Service provides transaction history and transaction search capabilities for retail banking customers.

Owner Team:
Digital Banking Team

Business Criticality:
High

Consumers:
- Online Banking Web Application
- Mobile Banking Application
- Customer Service Portal

Dependencies:

1. Core Banking Service
   Purpose:
   Retrieves account transaction records.

2. Customer Profile Service
   Purpose:
   Retrieves customer account ownership information.

3. Statement Service
   Purpose:
   Generates monthly statements.

4. Fraud Monitoring Service
   Purpose:
   Flags suspicious transactions.

5. Notification Service
   Purpose:
   Sends dispute status updates.

APIs:

GET /transactions

GET /transactions/search

GET /transactions/{transactionId}

POST /transactions/dispute

Database:
transaction_db

Tables:
- transactions
- transaction_categories
- dispute_requests

SLA:
99.95%

Average Daily Transactions:
12 Million

PCI DSS
SOX
